Frequently Asked Questions

What are some tips for potty training?
Be patient and encourage regular bathroom breaks.
How can I encourage healthy eating?
Involve them in cooking and offer a variety of healthy options.
How do I choose the right toys?
Choose toys that are age-appropriate and safe.
What age can I start reading to my child?
You can start reading to your child as early as infancy.
What is the best way to discipline my child?
Positive reinforcement and setting clear boundaries work best.
