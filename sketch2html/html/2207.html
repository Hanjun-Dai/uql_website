Frequently Asked Questions

What is a budget?
A budget is a plan that helps you manage your income and expenses.
How can I improve my credit score?
Pay your bills on time, reduce your debt, and check your credit report regularly.
What is the difference between saving and investing?
Saving is setting aside money for future use, while investing is using your money to generate returns.
What is compound interest?
Compound interest is interest calculated on the initial principal, which also includes all the accumulated interest from previous periods.
How do I create an investment portfolio?
You should assess your risk tolerance, consider diversification, and set clear financial goals.
