Frequently Asked Questions

Is it better to buy or rent?
It depends on your financial situation and lifestyle. Buying often builds equity while renting offers flexibility.
What are closing costs?
Closing costs are fees associated with finalizing a home purchase, typically ranging from 2% to 5% of the home's purchase price.
What should I look for in a neighborhood?
Consider factors such as schools, amenities, safety, and your commute when choosing a neighborhood.
How do I know how much I can afford?
Determine your budget by evaluating your income, debts, and savings. Consider getting pre-approved for a mortgage.
What is the process of buying a home?
The process generally involves finding an agent, searching for properties, making an offer, and closing the deal.
