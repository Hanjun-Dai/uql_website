Frequently Asked Questions

How can I ensure my child eats healthy?
Offer a variety of fruits and vegetables and involve them in meal planning.
What is the best age to start reading to my child?
Starting as early as possible is ideal to promote literacy.
How to manage screen time effectively?
Set clear limits and encourage other activities like reading or outdoor play.
What are the benefits of outdoor play?
Outdoor play promotes exercise, creativity, and social skills.
