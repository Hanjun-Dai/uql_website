Frequently Asked Questions

What is the scientific method?

The scientific method is a systematic way of learning about the world around us.

Why is science important?

Science helps us understand natural phenomena and improves our quality of life.

How can I get involved in science?

You can get involved by joining local science clubs, participating in workshops, or volunteering in research projects.

What are some key areas of scientific research?

Key areas include biology, chemistry, physics, environmental science, and space exploration.

Where can I find reliable scientific information?

Look for information from reputable sources such as academic journals, university websites, and established science organizations.
