Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process of buying a home?

The home buying process involves getting pre-approved, searching for a property, making an offer, and closing the sale.

How do I sell my property?

To sell your property, you need to prepare it for sale, list it with a real estate agent, and negotiate with potential buyers.

What are closing costs?

Closing costs include fees and expenses associated with buying or selling a property, such as loan origination fees, title insurance, and more.

What is homeowners insurance?

Homeowners insurance provides financial protection against damages to your home and personal property, as well as liability protection.
