How can I get involved in scientific research?
You can get involved by pursuing education in science, volunteering in labs, or joining citizen science projects.
Frequently Asked Questions
What is science?
Science is the pursuit of knowledge about the natural world through observation and experimentation.
What careers are available in science?
Careers in science include roles in research, education, healthcare, engineering, and many more.
What are the branches of science?
The main branches of science include physics, chemistry, biology, earth science, and astronomy.
How can I stay updated with scientific discoveries?
You can stay updated by following scientific journals, websites, and attending conferences.
