Frequently Asked Questions

How can I encourage healthy eating habits?
Introduce a variety of foods and make mealtime fun and inclusive.
What should I do if my child has a tantrum?
Stay calm, validate their feelings, and offer comfort and distraction.
What is positive discipline?
It's a discipline method that focuses on teaching and guiding rather than punishing.
What is the best age to start parenting classes?
Generally, it's beneficial to start classes during pregnancy or early childhood.
How can I create a safe environment for my child?
Childproofing your home and keeping sharp objects out of reach is essential.
