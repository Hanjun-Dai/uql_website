Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

How can I make a booking?

You can make a booking directly on our website or by contacting our customer service team.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer travel insurance options during the booking process.

Can I modify my booking after it's confirmed?

Yes, you can modify your booking by contacting our support team. Additional fees may apply.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy allows you to cancel up to 24 hours before your scheduled departure for a full refund.
