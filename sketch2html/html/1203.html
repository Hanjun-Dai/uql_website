Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before departure for a full refund.
What is included in the travel package?
Our packages typically include accommodation, meals, and transportation.
How do I change my booking?
To change your booking, contact our customer service team.
Are flights included in the package?
Flights are not included unless specified in the package details.
Do you offer travel insurance?
Yes, travel insurance can be added during the booking process.
