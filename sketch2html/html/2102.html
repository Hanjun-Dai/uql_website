Frequently Asked Questions

What are the steps to buy a house?

1. Get pre-approved for a mortgage.
2. Find a real estate agent.
3. Start house hunting.
4. Make an offer.
5. Close the deal.

How do I sell my home?

1. Determine your home's value.
2. Prepare your home for sale.
3. List your home with an agent.
4. Negotiate offers.
5. Close the sale.

What is a real estate agent?

A real estate agent is a licensed professional who helps buyers and sellers navigate real estate transactions.

What is the importance of credit score?

A credit score helps determine your eligibility for a mortgage and affects interest rates.
