Frequently Asked Questions

What is peer review?
Peer review is a process in which experts evaluate research before it is published to ensure quality and credibility.
Why is science important?
Science is crucial for advancing knowledge, driving innovation, and addressing challenges such as climate change and health issues.
What are the branches of science?
The main branches of science include physical sciences, biological sciences, and social sciences.
What is the scientific method?
The scientific method is a systematic approach to understanding the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis.
How can I contribute to science?
You can contribute by conducting research, participating in citizen science projects, or supporting scientific organizations.
